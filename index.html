How it works

Three small steps, one Florilège forever.

You set the scene, their friends fill the pages, and the book waits — sealed — for the day they open it.

Make the cover

Pick their name, their year, and the flower they love. Watch the cover bloom as you type.

Then

Friends add pages

Share one link. Everyone presses in their own page — a message, a photo, even a voice note.

Finally

They open it

Give them a secret key. They turn the pages and find the whole class waiting inside.
